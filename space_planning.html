SPACE PLANNING 

“Just like any piece of furniture or the color of the walls, Space Planning makes a huge difference in the design of the room.”

Space is one of the most important elements of interior design. Space acts as a foundation on which the entire interior design plan is built. At Eclectic, our first job is to create and transform spaces to tell our client’s story. Upon proper space planning, rests the goodness of interior design including the beauty quotient of the place.  If the room fails to meet the functionality – it really won’t matter how beautiful it looks. We make use of all the available space in an effective and efficient manner according to your requirements to maximize and improve the function and the value of your property.
