Hello, I’m Vignesh, Designer and Founder, Eclectic Design & Decor. We create designs with Knowledge and Passion. We are an young team who believe in the ability of Art, Architecture and Design thinking to effect good in the world.

What is Eclectic?

“Methods, beliefs, ideas, etc. that seems the best or most useful derived from a broad and diverse range of systems, rather than following a single system.”

Approach

At eclectic we strive to gain perspective on all of the different ways, to finally find the one design that meets our objective. We design spaces by keeping in mind of the customer’s space, their purpose, and their lifestyle. As every customer is a part of our team we understand that every one of them is unique and so are their needs. This thorough research and understanding, allows us to get to the pinnacle of their dream for a comfortable space.

Vision

“Design décor and thoughts for Functional and Artistic spaces.” Our projects are thoughtfully designed to make the product not for the product but for the result, for the profit of human being. Our main source of inspiration always remains the wellness of the world.
